Cookies

Description

This chocolate chip cookie recipe makes dozens of deliciously chewy cookies that the whole family will love

These cookies will make the whole family begging for more.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Baking Soda
  • Water
  • Salt
  • All-Purpose Flour
  • Chocolate Chips
  • Nuts (optional)

Steps

  1. Beat the butter and sugars, then beat in the eggs and vanilla.
  2. Dissolve the baking soda in hot water and add to the mixture.
  3. Stir in the flour, chocolate chips, and walnuts.
  4. Drop dough onto a prepared baking sheet.
  5. Bake until the edges are golden brown.
