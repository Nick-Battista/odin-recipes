Yogurt Parfait

Description

Looking for an amazingly delicious and healthy breakfast?

Then try these super amazing Yogurt Parfaits!

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups Vanilla Yogurt
  • 1 Cup Granola
  • 8 Blackberries

Steps

  1. Layer 1 cup yogurt, 1/2 cup granola, and 4 blackberries in a large glass; repeat layers.
